My name is Leslie Robertson
and I am a web developer and designer.

After I graduated from Parsons School of Design, I worked for Martha Stewart for 3.5 years, designing all kinds of holiday merchandise products. During my time there, I also oversaw part of a massive file-archiving project through which I found a serious love for databases and information management.

I took the plunge and quit my day job to pursue a career in tech down in Austin, TX, and ever since I've been developing websites and designing branding packages with Dos Mundos Creative.

Recently, I’ve been taking courses with Austin Coding Academy to enhance my coding abilities, and I’ve also been working with a friend/mentor to develop a static web-app on S3.
